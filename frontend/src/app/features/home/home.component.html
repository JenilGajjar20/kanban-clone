TaskFlow is a simple and intuitive Kanban board application designed to help individuals manage their personal tasks and stay organized. Whether you're working on personal projects, daily to-do lists, or long-term goals, TaskFlow offers an easy-to-use platform to visualize your workflow and track progress.

With its clean interface and drag-and-drop functionality, you can easily create tasks, categorize them, and move them through different stages of completion.
